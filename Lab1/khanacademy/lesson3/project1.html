Plov

  • Time: 90-120 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Carrot 2 pieces
Meat 500 g
Onion 1 piece
Rice 300 g
Water 450-500 ml
Salt & spices optional

Step 1: Peel the carrots and the onion.

Step 2: Wash the vegetables. Also wash the rice.

Step 3: Slice up the carrot and onion. Also slice up the meat.

Step 4: Prepare a pan or a kazan. Warm up the vegetable oil.

Step 5: Fry the carrot and onion together until they become golden yellow for 4-5 minutes.

Step 6: Add meat and cook until the outside is brown for 7-8 minutes

Step 7: Add rice and water, add salt and spices and mix everything up.

Step 8: Close the lid and wait for 50-60 minutes and you're done. Bon appetite!
